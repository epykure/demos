About Epyk
Epyk target is to allow light and advanced Python developers to give a new dimension to their scripts. Thanks to this library, they will be able to generate rich dashboard or web pages to share and integrate to the most popular web frameworks. From global enterprises to early-stage startups and small businesses, people everywhere use Epyk to improve time to market and team collaboration.
